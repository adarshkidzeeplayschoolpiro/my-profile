ABOUT US

 
Kidzee is one of India's leading preschool chains, focused on early childhood care and education for children between approximately 1.5 to 6 years of age. It offers Playgroup, Nursery, Junior KG, and Senior KG programs designed to support a child's overall development through activity-based and experiential learning.


Key Features of Kidzee

Child-centered learning approach.

Focus on cognitive, language, social, emotional, and physical development.

Interactive activities such as storytelling, music, dance, art, games, and outdoor play.

Phonics, early literacy, numeracy, and school readiness programs.

Safe, nurturing, and engaging learning environment.

Curriculum aligned with India's National Education Policy (NEP 2020) and National Curriculum Framework (NCF).
