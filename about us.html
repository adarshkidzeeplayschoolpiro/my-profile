ABOUT US

 
Kidzee is one of India's leading preschool chains, focused on early childhood care and education for children between approximately 1.5 to 6 years of age. It offers Playgroup, Nursery, Junior KG, and Senior KG programs designed to support a child's overall development through activity-based and experiential learning.


Key Features of Kidzee

Child-centered learning approach.

Focus on cognitive, language, social, emotional, and physical development.

Interactive activities such as storytelling, music, dance, art, games, and outdoor play.

Phonics, early literacy, numeracy, and school readiness programs.

Safe, nurturing, and engaging learning environment.

Curriculum aligned with India's National Education Policy (NEP 2020) and National Curriculum Framework (NCF).

Admissions & Enrollment process


    Admissions & Enrollment Process

Adarsh Kidzee Play School, Piro

We welcome parents to become a part of the Adarsh Kidzee family and give their children a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Admission Process

Step 1: Admission Enquiry

Visit the school or contact us for admission information.
Parents can discuss programs, fees, facilities, and daycare options.

Step 2: School Visit & Campus Tour

Parents and children are invited to visit the school.
Explore classrooms, activity areas, and learning facilities

Step 3: Registration Form Submission

Fill out the admission form with the child's details.
Choose the appropriate program based on age.

Step 4: Parent-Child Interaction

A friendly interaction may be conducted to understand the child's comfort level and developmental needs.

No formal written test or examination is required 

Step 5: Document Verification Submit the required documents for verification.

Step 6: Fee Payment & Enrollment Confirmation
Complete the admission formalities.

Receive confirmation and welcome kit details.
